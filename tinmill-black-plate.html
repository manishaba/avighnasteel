About Tin Mill Black Plate

Tin Mill Black Plate (TMBP) steel is manufactured from mostly from MR Grade Hot Rolled Coils . HR Coils are pickled to remove the surface oxide which are generated during the Hot Rolling Process , and further HR Coils are cold-reduced through a Cold Rolling Mill to the desired thickness. TMBP produces a low ductility product that is usually processed further to develop desired End Use characteristics. Further reprocessing steps like Annealing , Temper Rolling , Tension Leveling for formability , surface texture and, flatness are respectively undertaken as per requirements. TMBP are mainly used for general line cans, automotive and construction that require malleability. TMBP are highly suited for manufacturing of Tinplate and Tin free steel due to its excellent flatness and uniform thickness which is achieved through either single reduced or double reduced process.
